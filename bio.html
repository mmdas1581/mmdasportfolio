Artist Biography

I am a multidisciplinary visual artist mapping the topographies of extraction and irreversible ecological shifts. Born in Palakkad, Kerala, and currently based in New Delhi, my practice extracts the material memory of manufactured geologies through site-specific spatial installations, lens-based archiving, performance art, and sequential graphic interventions, by deployment of scavenged institutional debris and mass-produced artifacts. Drawing upon an embodied history of physical labour as a delivery agent, postman, and labourer in Palakkad, Kerala, my work documents the transactional archetypes of invisible workforces navigating these exhausted landscapes. I hold a BFA in Sculpture from Sree Sankaracharya University of Sanskrit and am currently pursuing an MFA in Sculpture at Jamia Millia Islamia.
