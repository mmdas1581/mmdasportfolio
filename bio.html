Artist Biography

Manu M Das is a multidisciplinary artist working across spatial installation, performance, lens-based documentation and sculptural intervention. His practice looks at ecological damage, material memory and the politics of invisible labour: what urban expansion costs, and who absorbs that cost without record or recognition.

Before formal art education, Das worked as a delivery agent, postman and construction labourer in Palakkad. That lived experience sits inside the work. He completed his BFA in Sculpture from Sree Sankaracharya University of Sanskrit, Kalady in 2024, and his MFA in Sculpture at Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi in 2026.

In 2026 he received the PJMT Saroj Jain Sculpture Award for Excellence in MFA Sculpture and was selected for the Inlaks Foundation Mentorship Program at Arthshila Delhi, as well as the Young Artists Mentorship Lab at Sameksha Art Gallery.
