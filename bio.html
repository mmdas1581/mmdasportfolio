Artist Biography

I am a visual artist based in New Delhi, originally from Palakkad, Kerala. My practice is rooted in my direct, physical experience within commercial supply chains-from growing up in a family shop to labouring as a construction worker, postman, and delivery agent. I do not observe the city from the outside; I understand it through the exhausting friction of moving its goods. In my studio practice, I investigate the hidden logistics of urban spaces, focusing on how the city consumes and discards. I specifically explore the "material memory" of everyday, mass-produced items. Operating as both a researcher and a labourer, I track the commercial routes of vulnerable, daily-use objects, and then physically pit them against abrasive industrial remnants. Through installations and rigorous material tests, I capture the exact moment an object is drained of its history and becomes waste, creating a physical record of Delhi's relentless cycle of use and disposal.
